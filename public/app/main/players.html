More About Our Channel.

Starting in Salt Lake City, UT, five friends gather to create content online. All of our members attended the same college together and quickly became friends. After college they all moved into the same apartment complex and decided to create YouTube videos together.

With the players diverse skills they were able to quickly put together a logo and name thus creating Players: 6. Now some may wonder where the 6th player is. The 6th Player is whoever decides to join temporarily! In our videos its common to include another person not part of the channel so stay tuned!
