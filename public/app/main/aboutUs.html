What is Players 6?

Players 6 is a collaborative and fun, game focused YouTube channel. It features 5 players, with an illusive 6th player who will be a random friend of ours to spice it up once in a while. We hope to bring something new and exciting to the YouTube gaming community. We'd like to put the fun back into it, in contrast to what has been the status quo.
